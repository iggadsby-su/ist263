Aries

The Ram: March 21-April 19

The first sign of the zodiac, Aries, is a fire sign, who loves to be first in anything they do. Associated with the body part of the head, Aries are known for diving into things head first, reflected in their go getter attitudes, and tendency to to be quick tempered. Aries is ruled by the planet of mars, which can be seen in their direct and reactive nature. Similar to the other fire signs, Aries are passionate and confident leaders. They are quick and blunt, and do not like to get caught up in the details of things. Aries are the baby of the zodiac, which is reflected in their impulsive nature, and blind buoyancy. This tends to also make them very playful and positive people. With a great deal of self-determination, they can get caught up in selfish tendencies.


Taurus

The Bull: April 20-May 20

The second sign of the zodiac is Taurus, an earth sign, well known for their obsession with luxury and comfort. Taurus is ruled by the planet of venus, meaning these bulls love everything to do with beauty, love, and aesthetics. They are known for being ambitious and focused, hard workers, who value and save their money to treat themselves to the finer things in life. Like other earth signs, they are associated with their loyal nature, and dedication to completing, and doing a good job in whatever they put themselves up too. However, this loyalty can often cross the line to stubbornness, as they pride themselves in always being right.


Gemini

The Twins: May 21-June 20

The third sign of the Zodiac is Gemini, an air sign, known for their infatuation with knowledge. Gemini’s are in a constant pursuit to learn more, and love the spread and share of information through conversation. Ruled by the planet of mercury, they are great conversationalists, and could talk to anyone, even a wall, for hours. However, this can leave them saying things before they really think about what they’re vocalizing. But don’t worry, they aren’t easily embarrassed and are good at moving on, quickly. They like to know a little bit about everything and can easily be recognized by the amount of questions they ask. Geminis love to try new things, and are always exploring new hobbies, or making new friends, though their short attention spans, leave them becoming easily bored with things.


Cancer

The Crab: June 21-July 22

The fourth sign of the Zodiac, Cancer, is a water sign, known for being highly attuned to their environment. These people are very protective of themselves, like the shell of a crab, Cancers tend to put up a cold demeanor to shield themselves. However, once they let you in they possess a very gentle nature, known for being caring people who are highly compassionate, and loyal friends. Ruled by the moon, comfort, and maternal energies are common themes seen within this sign. They prioritize making a comfortable and safe home environment for themselves, and would prefer a night in over a night out. Likely the mom of the friend group, they are known for embodying care taker rolls, but have to be careful as there is a fine line between caring for someone, and becoming possessive. Cancers avoid conflict and do not like tension, but if you ever do run into a problem, they lack confrontational skills, and tend to be passive aggressive.


Leo

The Lion: July 23-August 22

The fifth sign of the zodiac is Leo, a fire sign who loves the spotlight and drama of life. Leos are known for their theatrical personalities, and similar to other fire signs, are full of passion. The lion of the zodiac, Leos are natural leaders, who have no problem taking the credit for their teams work. Leo is ruled by the sun, which is only fitting as the sun is the center of attention, as all the other planets revolve around it. In friendships and relationships, they are known for their undivided loyalty and consistency, putting their hearts into everything they do, which only makes sense as the sign of Leo rules the heart. However, these lions can become blinded by the spotlight, and get caught up in their pride and ego. They tend to become jealous if they fear that they are being out shined. Above all else, Leos are known for their bravery. These people are incredibly strong and will go to great lengths to fulfill their ambitions, as failure is never an option for a Leo.


Virgo

The Virgin: August 23-September 22

The sixth sign of the zodiac is the earth sign, Virgo, known for their dedication to perfection, and unmatched eye for detail. This hyper-awareness to detail, tends to make them logical and systemic thinkers. Similarly to Gemini, Virgo is ruled by mercury, the planet of communication, however it manifests very differently. Virgo focuses on processing information so things are made very clear. Virgos obsession with perfectionism and always doing better, can pose problematic in their self concept and relationships. They have to realize that beauty can exist within imperfection, otherwise they will drive themselves crazy, as themselves and others will never be able to live up to their expectations. Virgos are hard workers who love to help. They are supportive friends who want to help people solve their problems in a practical means. The biggest lesson Virgo needs to learn is that sometimes things are good the way they are and there is not always a problem in need of fixing.


Libra

The Scales: September 23-October 22

The seventh sign of the Zodiac is Libra, an air sign, represented by the scales, these people are always seeking justice and known for prioritizing balance and harmony over everything. However, because of their tendency to consider all sides of the situation, these people tend to be very indecisive. Similar to Taurus, Libra is ruled by the planet of Venus, infatuating them will all things love and beauty. Libras are known for their good taste, whether its demonstrated through their fashion sense, or interior design, they have an eye for what looks good. Natural people pleasers, Libras strive to make everyone happy, but this can cause troubles in relationships if Libras find them self flirting with or entertaining people outside of who their loyalties lie with. They are talented socializers as they possess the ability to navigate any social situation with grace, whether it is a social gathering or conversation to settle a dispute.


Scorpio

The Scorpion: October 23-November 21

The eighth sign of the Zodiac, is the water sign, Scorpio, commonly known for their mysterious aura, these people can be hard to read when they have not let you in yet. A highly ambitious sign, these people are known for their emotional strength and intuition. Scorpios take their time and tend to play the long game, always planning several steps ahead, and never revealing their cards, in order to achieve what they are striving for. However, they can not let their ambitions over take them, otherwise they will manifest into an overarching need for control. Similar to Cancer, it is very hard to gain the trust of a Scorpio, but once you have it they demonstrate unmatched amounts of commitment and empathy to their relationships.


Sagittarius

The Archer: November 22-December 21

The ninth sign of the Zodiac, and the final fire sign, is Sagittarius, natural adventure seekers, this sign loves change and experiencing new things. Similar to Gemini, they are always seeking out new information, and experiences, making them above any other sign, the life of the party. These people are great story tellers, and entertainers, often known for being funny, they are skilled at captivating the attention of any group. However, they are known for being incredibly blunt, and their honesty can get them in trouble if it is at the expense of others feelings. But that doesn’t stop them, because their care free attitudes mean that they tend not to take anything too seriously, because to them, it was never that deep. They are ruled by the planet of Jupiter which represents abundance. This comes as no surprise as these people are always looking for the next best thing, and when they find something that intrigues them, they learn everything they can about it. That is until they become bored, as Sagittarius biggest challenge is consistency and sticking with routine.


Capricorn

The Sea-Goat: December 22-January 19

The tenth sign of the Zodiac, is Capricorn, an earth sign known for their hustle. If a Capricorn wants something, they are going to get it. Incredibly ambitious, this sign will not stop till they achieve what they set out for, no matter how many obstacles they have to overcome in the process, they are truly resilient. Due to their unmatched determination, Capricorns can come off as cold, and unemotional, but that’s because of their tendency to look at the big picture. Capricorns know that if something won’t matter 3 years down the line, then it is a waste of their time to care about it now, because of this they tend to lack sympathy for others problems. Ruled by the planet of Saturn, which is concerned with rules and time, Capricorns are very punctual and know how to organize their schedules to conquer what needs to be done in the time allotted. Said to age backwards, hidden beneath the surface of every Capricorn is a playful trouble maker, though it can be hard to tell as they come off as quite restrained.


Aquarius

The Water Bearer: January 20-February 18

The eleventh house of the Zodiac, is Aquarius, a trendsetting air sign who places great value on their individualism. Natural rebels, this sign is known for its innovative thinking and humanitarian ideals. Aquarians are motivated to make a change for the greater good. Strong proponents of team work and collaboration, they strive to make a positive change in the world around them. Known for being big thinkers, these people tend to get caught up in their visions of change, and in turn become distant in relationships. Moreover, these people can be stubborn and tend to have trouble looking at things from multiple perspectives, as they become fixated on what they believe to be right or best. Aquarians don’t do well with authority, as their obsession with individualism never allows them to follow the status quo. However, this leads them to be great agents of change, as they are often the people setting trends, but of course by the time it's trendy, they are already on to something new.


Pisces

The Fish: February 19-March 20

The twelfth and final sign of the Zodiac, is Pisces, a water sign, known for being the dreamers of the zodiac, these people always have their head in the clouds. More so than all other water signs, Pisces are truly known for their unmatched, almost physic intuition, and unwavering compassion and empathy. They have a kind nature to them. These people are highly sensitive, and can easily become overwhelmed by their own and others emotions, so it is important for them to stay grounded. Pisces is ruled by Neptune, the planet of creativity and dreams, giving these people large imaginations. However, this can cause them trouble as they prefer to look at the world through rose colored glasses. They need to learn that they can’t sugar coat everything, and avoiding conflict doesn’t make it disappear, and instead can translate as delusion. Pisces are masters at adapting to their environment, as their intuition gives them a clear sense of the vibes of a crowd.
